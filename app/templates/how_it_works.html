Blockchain Technology

FundForge uses the Polygon blockchain to ensure:

  • Secure and transparent transactions
  • Low transaction fees
  • Fast transaction processing
  • Smart contract automation

Getting Started

To get started with FundForge, you'll need:

  1. A MetaMask wallet (or compatible Web3 wallet)
  2. Some MATIC tokens for transaction fees
  3. A clear project or cause to fund
